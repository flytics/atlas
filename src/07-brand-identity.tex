\section*{Purpose}

This document defines the official Flytics brand identity system for product interfaces, website pages, pitch materials, and operational artifacts so all teams present a consistent visual and verbal identity.

\section{Brand Positioning}

\begin{longtable}{>{\bfseries}p{0.26\textwidth}p{0.64\textwidth}}
\toprule
Brand character & Precise, reliable, operational, and technically credible. \\
Brand promise & Convert drone mission complexity into clear, trusted, decision-ready outcomes. \\
Tone of voice & Clear, factual, confident, and low-noise. \\
Avoid & Hype language, playful visuals, excessive gradients, and decorative complexity. \\
\bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\section{Logo System}

\subsection{Primary Mark}

The primary logo should combine:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item a drone-inspired geometric symbol,
    \item a frame or layered-grid motif to reflect frame-level analysis,
    \item the wordmark `Flytics` in a clean sans-serif form.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Logo Variants}

\begin{longtable}{>{\bfseries}p{0.24\textwidth}p{0.66\textwidth}}
\toprule
Variant & Usage rule \\
\midrule
Primary horizontal logo & Default for website header, proposals, and product splash screens. \\
Symbol-only mark & Favicon, app launcher tile, and compact navigation surfaces. \\
One-color dark & Use on light backgrounds where color contrast must be minimal. \\
One-color light & Use on dark backgrounds or imagery overlays. \\
\bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\subsection{Clear Space and Size}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Minimum clear space: at least the height of the letter `F` on all sides.
    \item Minimum digital size for full logo: 120 px width.
    \item Minimum digital size for symbol-only icon: 20 px width.
\end{itemize}

\section{Icons8 Icon Standard}

\subsection{Approved Icon Style}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Primary icon style: \textbf{Material Rounded} from Icons8.
    \item Alternate style for mobile-first surfaces: \textbf{iOS 17 Glyph} if needed.
    \item Do not mix style packs within the same interface surface.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Icon Categories for Flytics}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Mission and drone: drone, route, radar, map pin.
    \item Data and frames: layers, grid, timeline, file stack.
    \item Operations and trust: shield check, warning, checklist, audit log.
    \item Finance and account: invoice, wallet, card, subscription.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Icon Usage Rules}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Default icon size: 20 px in dense UI, 24 px in standard UI.
    \item Use a single stroke or fill behavior per screen cluster.
    \item Pair icons with text for non-obvious actions.
    \item Keep icon color tied to semantic or neutral tokens.
\end{enumerate}

\section{UI Application Rules}

\begin{longtable}{>{\bfseries}p{0.24\textwidth}p{0.66\textwidth}}
\toprule
Surface & Brand rule \\
\midrule
Landing page (`www`) & Lead with trust and operational proof, not decorative hero treatment. \\
Client portal (`app`) & Use high-clarity forms, status timelines, and restrained visual noise. \\
Pilot portal (`pilot`) & Emphasize eligibility, opportunity clarity, and task progression. \\
Operations console (`ops`) & Prioritize data density, risk visibility, and decision controls. \\
Billing and admin & Use conservative visual hierarchy and auditable action design. \\
\bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\section{Do and Do Not}

\begin{longtable}{>{\bfseries}p{0.48\textwidth}p{0.48\textwidth}}
\toprule
Do & Do not \\
\midrule
Use consistent typography and icon style across all portals. & Mix multiple icon packs on one screen. \\
Use semantic color for state and risk communication. & Use accent colors as random decoration. \\
Design for readability in table-heavy operational screens. & Overuse card-heavy marketing layouts in operations UIs. \\
Keep interactions explicit with clear labels and status text. & Hide critical actions behind ambiguous icon-only controls. \\
\bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\end{enumerate}
