\section*{How to Read This Document}

These projections are revenue estimates only. They extend the original conservative business case into a three-year planning model that includes AI-enabled premium analytics, recurring monitoring subscriptions, and value-added reporting services. Costs, gross margin, capital requirements, cash timing, taxes, and financing should be modeled separately before investment approval.

\section{Projection Method}

The target scenario assumes Flytics launches with the original baseline revenue streams and adds AI-driven offers that expand average deal value and repeat business. The model separates revenue into five streams so each can be validated later with real demand, pricing, conversion, attachment, retention, and margin data.

\begin{longtable}{p{0.30\textwidth}r p{0.34\textwidth}}
\toprule
\textbf{Revenue stream} & \textbf{Year 1 assumption} & \textbf{Growth logic} \\
\midrule
New pilot contracts and drone leases & \$357,500 & 12\% growth in Year 2 and 10\% in Year 3 as the pilot network expands. \\
Drone flight services and data analysis & \$565,000 & 18\% growth in Year 2 and 16\% in Year 3 from demand growth, better conversion, and improved assignment speed. \\
AI analytics premium services & \$84,750 & 15\% attachment against service revenue in Year 1, expanding with adoption and standardized analytics packages. \\
Recurring monitoring subscriptions & \$72,000 & Initial recurring clients with stronger expansion in Years 2 and 3 as monitoring workflows mature. \\
Priority turnaround and custom reporting & \$24,000 & Small Year 1 add-on revenue with steady attachment growth. \\
\bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\section{Key Takeaways}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item The original business case supports a Year 1 base of \$922,500 before AI-specific upsell and recurring offers are included.
    \item In the target case, total revenue rises to \$1,103,250 in Year 1, \$1,333,350 in Year 2, and \$1,581,530 in Year 3.
    \item The largest expansion opportunity is the combination of premium analytics, subscriptions, and operational improvements that increase attachment and repeat business.
    \item Recurring monitoring contracts and AI analytics become increasingly important by Year 3, improving predictability and differentiation.
\end{itemize}
